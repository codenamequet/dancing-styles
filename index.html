What is Dancin Styles Fitness?

Dancing Styles Fitness is ran by Temi Roberts, your certified zumba instructor who has always had a passion for dance and is now offering zumba classes with various world rhythms from African, Caribbean and Latin music that'll keep you moving and melting the calories away. She is also gearing up to offer some fun, lusty, and vibrant dancehall and afrobeats dance classes that you'll be sure to love.

"My mission is to help others focus on the vast benefits of dancing while building a community of people who can share positive vibes and energy".
